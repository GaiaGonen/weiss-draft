Who are we

Founded in Tel Aviv in 2011, WeissBeerger is a Tech company for the Food, Beverage, and Retail Industries. Made up of a team of industry experts, AI and machine learning engineers, and data scientists, today, after having been acquired by Anheuser-Busch Inbev (BUD), Weissbeerger serves as the analytics and innovation arm of the largest brewery in the world. With over 500K customers operating across 3 continents, we impact the lives of millions of bar, restaurant, and store owners.

What we do

WeissBeerger has developed the leading data analytics solution for the Food & Beverage, and Retail Industries, enabling our customers to optimize the way they run their business by leveraging the advantages of a data-driven world.

The BEES platform is a B2B marketplace that enables retailers to purchase all of their goods and stock items from a single online shop.

Once the BEES platform has gotten to know the customer, it will provide them with data and insights, as well as transactional and educational resources that will allow them to reduce the overhead of their day-to-day operations, and increase their bottom line.

Today, more than 700,000 small and medium-sized retailers, across 18 countries, utilize the BEES platform as part of their daily routine.

Field of expertise

Data science innovation

Our vision is to digitize the retail industry by giving our customers data based knowledge, insights, and tools with which they can better run and manage their business.

The Data Science & Innovation division allows us to realize our vision by inventing, developing, and customizing capabilities and tools for small and medium businesses in the retail and food & beverage industries. Leveraging unique data, and combining it with our data science intelligence, commercial mindset, and business savvy, we help our customers grow and optimize their business by making smarter purchases and more sales.
